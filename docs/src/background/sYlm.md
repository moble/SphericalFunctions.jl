# [``{}_{s}Y_{ℓ,m}`` functions](@id background_sYlm)

The [previous page](@ref background_differential_operators) introduced
the left and right Lie derivative operators acting on functions of a
quaternion argument.  The familiar way of arriving at the standard
(scalar) spherical harmonics is to consider functions that are
eigenfunctions of the *left* Lie derivative operators.
